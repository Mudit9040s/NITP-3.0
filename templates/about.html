About Us

This project is designed as a soil-management tool for farmers, homeowners, landscape contractors, golf-course superintendents, ornamental nurserymen and others interested in the fertility of their soil.
Each request is analyzed for pH, EC, Nitrogen, Phosphorus and Potassium contents as input. The final report includes the chemical analysis of the soil determining whether or not the soil is fertile for plantation.

The designed web-app also has a crop recommendation system which is aimed to suggest the possible crops that can be grown in soil depending upon some input parameters, namely pH, rainfall, humidity and temperature.
The team has also tried to put in important information like irrigation requirements, best time sow, best soil type, etc about various crops commonly grown by farmers in India.
